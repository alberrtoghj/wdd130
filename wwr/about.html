Our mission is to deliver thrilling, safe, and memorable white water rafting adventures, connecting people with nature while promoting teamwork and environmental respect

History

In the 1940s, commercial whitewater rafting emerged, primarily on the Colorado River, after World War II veterans began guiding expeditions. The sport continued to grow in popularity throughout the 1960s and 70s, as advances in raft materials made the activity safer and more accessible. Today, whitewater rafting is a popular recreational activity worldwide, attracting adventure-seekers to rivers of all classes, from beginner-friendly rapids to extreme, high-adrenaline experiences.
